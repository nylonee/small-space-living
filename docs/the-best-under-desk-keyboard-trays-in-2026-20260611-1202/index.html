Buying Guide

When choosing an under desk keyboard tray, consider the following factors:

  1. Size and Space Considerations: Ensure that the tray fits comfortably on your desk without obstructing other items.
  2. Adjustability: Look for trays with multiple height or tilt settings to accommodate different working styles.
  3. Installation Method: Decide if you prefer clamps, screws, or magnetic attachments based on your desk’s surface and personal preference.
  4. Budget: Set a budget range that fits your needs without sacrificing quality.

A: Yes, many under desk keyboard trays are designed to work with both seated and standing desks. However, ensure the weight capacity is suitable for your setup.

Q: Are there any maintenance tips for under desk keyboard trays?
