Buying Guide: What to Consider When Choosing a Storage Ottoman

  1. Size and Space: Measure the area where you plan to place the ottoman or bench. Choose a size that fits comfortably without overcrowding your space.
  2. Storage Capacity: Consider how much storage you need. Some may offer more capacity than others, so choose one based on your specific needs.
  3. Material Quality: Higher quality materials will typically last longer and provide a more comfortable sitting experience. Look for pieces made from durable fabrics or sturdy wooden constructions.
  4. Design and Aesthetics: Ensure the design complements the overall style of your home decor. You want furniture that is both functional and visually appealing.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a storage ottoman in my bedroom?

A: Absolutely! Many storage ottomans are designed with large hidden compartments perfect for keeping extra bedding, blankets, or seasonal clothing out of sight but easily accessible when needed.

Q: Are there any specific features to look for if you have small children?

A: Look for ottomans and benches that have child-safe materials. Some brands offer soft cushioning without excessive padding that could pose a risk to small children. Additionally, consider pieces with locks or latches on storage compartments to prevent accidental falls.

Q: How do I clean my storage ottoman or bench?

A: Regular cleaning is important to maintain the appearance and functionality of your storage furniture. Most can be wiped down with a damp cloth, but check the manufacturer’s instructions for specific care recommendations.
