FAQ

Q: What is the difference between a cable clip and a cable tie?

A: Cable clips are typically flexible and can be adjusted to fit various cable sizes. They offer more flexibility in how you manage your cables. Cable ties are often rigid with an adhesive backing, making them easier to apply but less adjustable once installed.

Q: Are these products compatible with all types of desks?

A: Most of the products listed here are designed to be adaptable and can fit most desk types. However, it’s always a good idea to check compatibility before purchasing, especially if your desk has unique features or materials.

Q: Can I reuse cable ties after removing them from my cables?

A: Yes, many reusable cable ties can be uninstalled without leaving sticky residue behind. They are designed to be re-applied multiple times, making them a cost-effective solution in the long run.

Q: How do I choose between under-desk and on-top cable management options?

A: Under-desk options like trays or boxes are better for hiding cables from view while keeping your desk surface clutter-free. On-top solutions can be useful if you want to manage cords that pass over the desk, but they may take up more space.

Q: What size of cable clips should I look for?

A: Look for clips with different sizes so you can accommodate various cable thicknesses. Most sets come with multiple sizes, allowing you to cater to your specific needs.
