What to Look for in Storage Ottomans and Benches

1. Size

In small spaces, size matters. Look for ottomans that are compact yet versatile enough to fit your needs without overwhelming the room. The ideal size will depend on the space you have. A general guideline is:
- For entryways or hallways: 24-30 inches in diameter.
- For living rooms and bedrooms: 36-45 inches.

2. Capacity

Storage capacity can be a game-changer, especially if you’re short on floor space. Opt for ottomans with at least 48 inches of storage depth or consider those that can hold up to 100 pounds (45 kg) or more. Some popular models include:
- SONGMICS 30-inch Folding Storage Ottoman Bench: With a capacity of 660 lbs, this bench is perfect for small entryways and provides ample space for shoes or miscellaneous items.
- SONGMICS 43-inch Storage Ottoman Bench: This large storage bench can accommodate more weight and offers extra seating, making it suitable for living rooms or bedrooms.

3. Features

Modern ottomans often come with additional features that enhance their functionality:
- Folding Mechanism: A folding mechanism allows the ottoman to double as a seat when needed.
- Removable Liners: These can be easily washed and replaced, maintaining hygiene and freshness.
- Adjustable Height: Some models offer adjustable heights for different uses, such as converting from a coffee table to an extra seating option.

4. Price Range

Storage ottomans and benches come in various price points, but it’s crucial to strike the right balance between quality and cost. Here are some typical ranges:
- Under £50: Ideal for budget-conscious buyers.
- £50-120: Offers a good mix of functionality and style.
- Over £120: High-end models with advanced features and materials.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Ignoring the Storage Capacity

While aesthetics are important, don’t overlook the primary function of these ottomans. Opting for a model with insufficient storage can defeat its purpose in small spaces.

2. Not Considering the Folding Mechanism

A folding mechanism is essential if you need to use the ottoman as both a storage piece and seating area. Ensure that the mechanism is smooth and easy to operate.

3. Overlooking Liner Quality

High-quality liners can extend the lifespan of your ottoman and make it easier to maintain hygiene, especially in households with children or pets.

4. Choosing Too Large a Bench for Small Spaces

While larger benches might seem like a better choice due to their storage capacity, they can take up too much space in smaller rooms, defeating the purpose of having an ottoman in the first place.

A Simple Decision Framework

  1. Assess Your Space: Determine how much space you have available and what specific needs you have (e.g., entryway shoes, extra seating).
  2. Identify Your Budget: Decide how much you are willing to spend on a storage ottoman or bench.
  3. Consider the Functionality: Think about whether you need a simple folding mechanism or additional features like adjustable height or removable liners.
  4. Check Reviews and Specifications: Look for detailed reviews, specifications, and user feedback before making a purchase decision.
