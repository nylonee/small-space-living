How to Choose the Best Under Desk Keyboard Trays for Your Small Space in 2026

When it comes to small spaces, every inch of desk real estate counts. One often-overlooked piece of furniture that can make a significant difference is an under desk keyboard tray. Not only do these trays save space and improve ergonomics, but they also help maintain a clutter-free workspace. In this guide, we’ll explore what to look for when choosing the best under desk keyboard tray for your small space in 2026.

What to Look For

Size

The size of your under desk keyboard tray should match the dimensions of your workspace and the needs of your setup. A smaller tray is perfect for compact desks, while larger models can accommodate multiple keyboards or even a mouse pad. In 2026, you might find trays ranging from 18x30cm to over 65x40cm, so it's essential to measure your desk space first.

Features

Key features to consider include:

  • Adjustable Height: Most trays come with height adjustment capabilities. Look for a model that allows for at least 12 settings, as this flexibility can cater to different user heights and preferences.
  • Ergonomic Design: Ensure the tray is designed to promote good posture. Features like anti-slip mats, comfortable grips, and smooth rolling mechanisms are beneficial.
  • Compatibility: Check if the tray supports various keyboard types, including wireless models, or whether it has a clamp-on design that can fit most desk surfaces.

Price Range

Under desk keyboard trays typically range from £20 to £150, with some premium options exceeding £300. In 2026, expect prices to fluctuate slightly due to market trends and technological advancements. For the average user, a tray within the £40-£70 range offers a good balance between functionality and cost.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Overlooking Ergonomics

Ergonomic considerations are crucial in reducing strain on your wrists and neck. A poorly designed tray can negate the benefits of having one at all.

Ignoring Desk Compatibility

Not all trays fit every desk, so ensure you measure carefully before purchasing. Some trays may require tools for installation or have specific attachment points that need to be considered.

Choosing a Tray Too Large

While larger trays offer more space, they might not fit small desks comfortably. Always measure your workspace and the tray together to avoid disappointment.

Simple Decision Framework

  1. Measure Your Desk: Determine how much space you can realistically allocate for an under desk keyboard tray.
  2. Assess Ergonomics Needs: Think about whether you need a more basic or advanced ergonomic design based on your posture needs.
  3. Check Compatibility: Ensure the model supports your current setup, including cable management if necessary.
  4. Consider Price Range: Set a budget and stick to it while prioritizing features that matter most.

By following these steps, you can choose an under desk keyboard tray that enhances your productivity and comfort without compromising on space in 2026.

Conclusion

Whether you're setting up a home office or upgrading your small space desk, investing in a quality under desk keyboard tray is a smart move. In 2026, prioritize functionality over flashiness—your back and wrists (and potentially your productivity) will thank you!
