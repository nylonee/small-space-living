Why You Need a Storage Ottoman in a Small Flat

Storage ottomans are the unsung heroes of small-space living. They give you a comfortable place to sit while hiding away everything from shoes and bags to blankets, books, and board games. The best part? They don't look like storage — they look like furniture. In a tiny flat, that's the difference between a space that feels cluttered and one that feels thoughtfully designed.

Whether you need an entryway bench where you can sit to put your shoes on (with hidden storage for said shoes), a living room ottoman that doubles as a coffee table and blanket stash, or a bedroom bench at the foot of your bed, there's an option for every need and budget.

How to Choose the Right Storage Ottoman

When shopping for a storage ottoman for your small flat, here's what to consider:

Size and placement — Measure your space before buying. A 30-inch ottoman fits most hallways and bedroom ends. The 43-inch version needs a bit more room but offers significantly more storage. Think about where you'll use it — entryway, living room, or bedroom.

Weight capacity — If you plan to sit on it regularly, look for a minimum 300 lbs capacity. The SONGMICS ottomans we recommend are rated at 660 lbs, so they're built to last.

Storage volume — Consider what you'll store. Shoes and small items work well in a 30-inch ottoman. Bulky items like duvets and winter coats need the 43-inch version or a dedicated storage bench.

Style — Most ottomans come in neutral colours (black, grey, beige) that blend with any decor. Consider the aesthetic of your room before choosing.
