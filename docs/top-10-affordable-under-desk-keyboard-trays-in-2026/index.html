FAQs

Q: What is the difference between an under desk keyboard tray and a regular keyboard tray?
A: An under desk keyboard tray is specifically designed to be placed beneath your desk rather than on top of it. This design helps save valuable desk space while providing additional surface area for your keyboard, mouse, or other accessories.

Q: How do I choose the right size for my under desk keyboard tray?
A: Measure the width and depth of your current work setup to ensure that the tray will fit comfortably beneath your desk. A larger tray may be necessary if you have a wide monitor or multiple accessories to organize.

Q: Can an under desk keyboard tray improve ergonomics?
A: Yes, many models offer adjustable height settings and can help reduce strain by keeping your wrists at a neutral position, which is especially beneficial for long hours of work.

Q: Is it easy to install an under desk keyboard tray?
A: Most trays come with clear instructions and are relatively straightforward to set up. Some may require tools or screws, while others have clamp-on designs that make installation quick and hassle-free.
