FAQ

Q: What is the difference between an under desk keyboard tray and a regular desk?
A: An under desk keyboard tray helps you save space by placing the keyboard below the desk surface, while a standard desk typically has the keyboard on top. Under desk trays are ideal for small spaces where every inch of desk real estate matters.

Q: Can I use an under desk keyboard tray with my existing desk?
A: Most models can be installed on most desks, but it’s important to check compatibility before purchasing. Some trays come with universal mounting kits that allow easy installation on various desk types.

Q: How much weight can these trays typically hold?
A: The majority of under desk keyboard trays have a weight capacity of around 5kg, which is sufficient for most setups. However, always verify the specific model's specifications before making a purchase.

Q: Are under desk keyboard trays easy to install?
A: Installation varies by model but many are designed to be user-friendly and can be set up in just a few minutes with basic tools. Models like the Targus ProDesk Under Desk Keyboard Tray have been noted for their simple installation process.
