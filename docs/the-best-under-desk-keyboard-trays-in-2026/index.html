Buying Guide

When choosing the right under desk keyboard tray, consider the following factors:
1. Size: Ensure the size of the tray is appropriate for your keyboard and mouse.
2. Adjustability: Look for trays with multiple height settings or electric adjustment mechanisms.
3. Compatibility: Check if the tray fits securely on your desk type.
4. Durability: Choose a model known for its sturdy construction to ensure it lasts longer.
5. Price: Consider your budget and determine how much you are willing to spend.
