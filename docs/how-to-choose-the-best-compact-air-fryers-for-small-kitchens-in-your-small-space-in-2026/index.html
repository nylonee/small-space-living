What to Look For

Size

Compact air fryers are designed with small spaces in mind. Opting for a model that fits comfortably within your kitchen or countertop is crucial. In 2026, the market offers various sizes ranging from 1.5 litres up to 5.7 litres. Consider how much food you typically prepare and choose a size accordingly.

Features

Key features to look out for include:

  • Cooking Functionality: Multi-functional air fryers can perform multiple cooking tasks like frying, roasting, baking, and reheating. This versatility saves space and reduces the need for additional appliances.
  • Ease of Cleaning: Non-stick interiors are ideal as they make cleaning easier without sacrificing performance. Some models also come with detachable parts for easy washing.
  • Control Panel & Display: Digital displays offer precise temperature control and time settings, while simple touch controls can be more user-friendly. Programmable options allow you to set your desired cooking parameters easily.

Price Range

In 2026, prices for compact air fryers generally range from £39.99 up to around £150 depending on the features and brand. When budgeting, consider a balance between functionality and cost. A mid-range model like the Ninja Air Fryer 3.8L AF100UK priced at around £79.99 offers excellent value.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Overlooking Size

Choosing an air fryer that’s too large can waste valuable counter space. Always measure your kitchen before making a purchase.

Ignoring Cleaning Ease

A compact air fryer with easy-to-clean parts will save you time and effort in the long run. Non-stick interiors and removable components are key features to look for.

Overlooking Cooking Functionality

Ensure that the model can handle all your cooking needs, whether it’s frying, baking, or reheating.

Simple Decision Framework

  1. Assess Your Space: Measure available kitchen space to determine the ideal size of an air fryer.
  2. Identify Needs: List out what you need the air fryer to do (e.g., frying, baking, reheating).
  3. Set a Budget: Determine how much you are willing to spend.
  4. Check Features: Prioritize features like multi-functionality, easy cleaning, and user-friendliness based on your needs.
  5. Read Reviews: Look at customer reviews for reliability and performance.
  6. Test the Model: If possible, try out a model before buying or return it if you’re not satisfied.
