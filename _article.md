## Why You Need an Under Desk Keyboard Tray

A keyboard tray mounted underneath your desk does more than just clear clutter. It enables proper ergonomic positioning: your elbows should sit at roughly 90 degrees with your forearms parallel to the floor, and the tray puts your keyboard and mouse at exactly that height. Without one, most people end up resting their wrists on the desk edge, which leads to strain over time.

In a small home office, an under desk tray also frees up the entire desk surface for writing, drawing, or a second monitor. That's a game-changer when your desk measures barely a metre across. The VIVO Large Under Desk Keyboard and Mouse Tray is a standout here, offering a generous 68x28cm workspace with swinging height adjustment and 12 different settings to get the angle just right. ## Buying Guide

When building your small home office setup with an under desk keyboard tray, here's what to consider:

**Desk thickness and clearance.** Measure the underside of your desk before buying any clamp-on product. Most trays and monitor arms accommodate desks up to 5-7cm thick, but always check the specs.

**Tray size.** The keyboard tray should be wide enough to hold both your keyboard and mouse side by side. The VIVO Large Under Desk Tray (68x28cm) is generous enough for full-size keyboards and mice.

**Height adjustability.** Look for a tray with tilt adjustment and height settings. Fixed trays can cause wrist strain if they're not at the perfect height.

**Weight capacity.** Check that your monitor arm can handle your screen's weight. The ErGear arm supports up to 9kg, covering most monitors up to 34 inches.

**Cable routing.** Plan your cable management before assembling everything. Run cables through trays first, then plug in, then bundle with VELCRO ties.

## FAQ

**Will an under desk keyboard tray work with any desk?**
Most trays use a clamp mechanism that works with standard desk edges up to about 5-7cm thick. Glass desks are not suitable. If you have an unusually thick or thin desk top, measure carefully and check the product specifications.

**Can I install a keyboard tray without drilling?**
Yes. The VIVO Large Under Desk Keyboard and Mouse Tray uses a clamp-on design that requires no drilling or permanent modification to your desk.

**Is a monitor arm difficult to install?**
Not at all. Both the ErGear and HUANUO monitor arms use a C-clamp or grommet mount and come with clear instructions. Most people can install one in under 15 minutes.

**Will a keyboard tray work with a standing desk converter?**
Yes. A keyboard tray mounted under a standing desk converter gives you the best of both worlds: your keyboard stays at the perfect height while the converter holds your monitors. The VIVO 26 inch or VIVO 36 inch desk converters pair well with this setup.

**What's the minimum desk depth for a keyboard tray?**
You need at least 10-12cm of clearance between the front edge of your desk and any obstructions underneath. Most standard desks — including IKEA tabletops — have this easily.

**How do I keep cables tidy with a keyboard tray?**
Use sticky-backed cable clips to route cables along the underside of your desk, and bundle excess length with VELCRO Brand Cable Ties. The Neatify Cable Management Box can hide power adapters and extension leads underneath.
